## Problem Statement

This dashboard helps CodeX, a German beverage company, understand the Indian market better. It aims to increase brand awareness, market share, and product development based on survey responses from 10,000 participants across 10 cities in India. The analysis focuses on demographics, consumer preferences, competition, market analysis, brand penetration, and purchase behavior.

### Steps Followed

- **Step 1:** Load data into Power BI Desktop.
- **Step 2:** Clean and preprocess the dataset to handle outliers and inconsistencies.
- **Step 3:** Create visualizations to represent various insights from the data.
  - Demographic distribution of respondents.
  - Consumer preferences for ingredients, packaging, and reasons for consumption.
  - Competitor analysis, including brand preference and desired improvements.
  - Market analysis and effectiveness of different marketing channels.
  - Purchase behavior, including typical consumption situations and preferred purchase locations.
- **Step 4:** Analyze key performance indicators (KPIs) such as demographic insights, consumer preferences, competition analysis, market analysis, brand penetration, and purchase behavior.
- **Step 5:** Write and publish report.

## Insights

A multi-page report was created on Power BI Desktop and then published. The following inferences can be drawn from the dashboard:

### Demographics
- **Age Distribution:** 55% of respondents fall within the 19-30 age group.
- **Gender Distribution:** 60% male, 35% female, 5% non-binary.
- **Target Audience:** 5520 respondents aged 15-30.

### Consumer Preferences
- **Favored Ingredients:** Caffeine (39%), Vitamins (25%), Sugar (20%), Guarana (16%).
- **Packaging Preferences:** Compact and portable cans are highly favored.
- **Reasons for Consumption:** Increased energy and focus (36%), combat fatigue (24%), enhance sports performance (16%).

### Competition Analysis
- **Market Leaders:** Cola Coka, Bepsi.
- **Brand Preference Factors:** Brand reputation, taste/flavor preference, availability.
- **Desired Improvements:** Reduced sugar content, more natural ingredients, wider range of flavors.

### Market Analysis
- **Effective Channels:** Online ads (40%), TV commercials (27%).
- **Brand Perception:** Neutral (59.7%), Positive (22.6%), Negative (17.7%).
- **Purchase Locations:** Supermarkets (45%), Online retailers (26%).

### Purchase Behavior
- **Typical Consumption Situations:** During sports/exercise, while studying/working late.
- **Price Preferences:** 50-99 INR range.

## Recommendations
- **Availability:** Expand product availability to reach a broader consumer base.
- **Health Concern Survey:** Conduct surveys to align product development with consumer health preferences.
- **Limited Edition Packaging:** Introduce limited edition packaging to attract consumers.
- **Targeted Marketing Campaigns:** Use social media, influencer marketing, and online retail advertising to increase brand visibility.
- **Brand Ambassador:** Consider a brand ambassador like Virat Kohli to connect with the target audience.
- **Target Audience Focus:** Concentrate on youth demographics through sponsorships of music festivals, raves, and sporting events.
